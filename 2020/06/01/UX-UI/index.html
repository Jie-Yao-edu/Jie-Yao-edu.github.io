UX stands for user experience. User experience refers to the user’s subjective feelings and satisfaction of needs when using and interacting with the product. UX reports are reasonable predictions and analyses that

user experience designers make between users and systems based on data, emotion, and user research.

  • Demographic


According to a survey report on part-time jobs of American college students released by China news service, since 2012, the proportion of part-time jobs among American college students has reached 73 percent, and by 2015, 85 percent of American college students are doing part-time jobs. The majority of students are willing to take a part-time job.


Contemporary college students to the two biggest part-time demand: earn living expenses and exercise ability. College students began to take the initiative to make plans for their careers and spare time. Making money is still one of the important purposes, and post-1995 college students are eager to put their knowledge into practice and make money with their majors.

Liu chuanyu, vice President of China’s leading website kuiwei, said most weike are still part-time, with the post-80s and post-90s generation accounting for more than 60 percent of the population. For many young students and professionals, it’s a great way to learn and test your abilities.

To sum up, our main target group is college students who have certain professional skills and want to exercise this ability so as to earn some living expenses.

WHY IS USER EXPERIENCE (UX) IMPORTANT?

There are three reasons below to explain this question.

  1. Increase customer satisfaction

    Enhance your customer satisfaction by improving the usability and pleasure of interacting with all touch points across platforms and devices.

  2. Understand your audiences

    By conducting UX research, you will gain insights into how a user behave and react to your products and services. The same website design can be just nice for users in the USA; not complicated enough for users in China or Japan; but too complicated for users in Indonesia at the same time.

    This difference in perception of user experience across countries and regions has prompted some global e-commerce brands to release different website designs catering to the specific markets.

  3. Good UX is good business

    An investment in UX sees a lower cost of customer acquisition, lower support cost, increased customer retention and increased market share.

    However, as a UX professional, you should be aware that design is more than appearance. Steve Jobs once said that design is not just what it looks like and feels like; design is how it works. UX design comprises UX elements from information architecture, visual design, interaction design, content strategy to sitemaps.

    After gaining more understanding of your users from research, you can first create a set of personas, storyboards, user journey map and ecosystem map.
